Giraffe

contrary to what most people assume, giraffes are divided into four distinct species. The northern giraffes, southern giraffes, reticulated giraffes, and the masai giraffes.

  • Hight: up to 5 m
  • Whait: up to 1900 kg
  • Age: Up to 26 years
  • Speed: 60 KM in short distances

The Giraffe is a large African mammal with a very long neck and forelegs, having a coat patterned with brown patches separated by lighter lines. It is the tallest living animal.
